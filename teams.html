Is Formula1 a Team Sport?

F1 is fundamentally a team sport, though the drivers are the public face of each team. They rely heavily on engineers, mechanics, and strategists. There are 10 teams, each team consists of 2 drivers. Some notable teams are Ferrari, Mercedes, McLaren, and RedBull Racing.
