Championships and Innovation

The races are held on circuits or closed roads. Each driver and team accumulate points based on their performance. Cars could race up to 370km/h, each team tries to gain an advatage by innovating and installing upgrades within the regulations. The drivers are also connected to their engineers through a radio channel which can be disabled and enabled. Notable World Champions of Formula 1 are Sebastian Vettel, Lewis Hamilton, Max Verstappen, and Michael Schumacher.
